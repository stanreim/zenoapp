# 🎯 Quick Interaction Guide

## First Time Use

1. **Click anywhere** → White screen shows subtle pulse hint
2. **Hold (don't release)** → Radial ring appears and fills
3. **Keep holding** → Numbers appear showing selected duration (15/30/45/60 min)
4. **Release** → Session starts immediately with video and audio

## During Session

### Play / Pause
- **Quick click** (< 600ms) → Toggles play/pause
- Audio fades in/out smoothly over ~1 second
- Video continues playing but audio stops

### Switch Moods
- **Long press** (> 600ms) → Cycles to next mood
- Video and audio crossfade over 2-3 seconds
- Cycles through 4 moods total: Mood 0 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 0

### Visual Timer Feedback

As time progresses (time remaining decreases):

| Time Remaining | Visual Change |
|---------------|---------------|
| 100% → 50% | Saturation decreases |
| 100% → 50% | Contrast decreases |
| 0% → 100% | Blur increases slightly |

No numbers are shown - you feel the time through visual degradation.

## Session End

When timer reaches zero:
- Audio fades to silence (3 seconds)
- Video fades to pure white (3 seconds)
- **Click anywhere** → Resets completely back to beginning

## Tips

✅ **DO**
- Use in full-screen mode for best experience
- Wear headphones for immersive audio
- Let your eyes defocus and peripheral vision take over
- Use long press to find your preferred mood
- Pause if you need to step away

❌ **DON'T**
- Don't expect visible timers or numbers
- Don't expect UI elements or buttons
- Don't try to right-click or use keyboard shortcuts
- Don't expect to see time remaining precisely

## Gesture Recognition

The app distinguishes between:
- **Quick click**: < 600ms press
- **Long press**: ≥ 600ms press

**Important**: If you accidentally trigger a long press when you meant to click, just wait - you can click again after releasing.

## Mobile Usage

On mobile:
- Works exactly the same
- Touch and hold = long press
- Quick tap = click
- Disable auto-lock for uninterrupted sessions
- Consider using Do Not Disturb mode

## Desktop Usage

On desktop:
- Click and hold with mouse
- Press `F11` for full-screen (recommended)
- Close other tabs for fewer distractions
- Consider using a second monitor for focus timer

## Troubleshooting Interactions

**Timer selector won't appear**
- Make sure you're clicking the white screen
- If you see video, you've already started - click to pause first

**Can't switch moods**
- Make sure you're holding for at least 600ms
- You should feel the long press (slight delay before mood switches)
- Long press only works during active session

**Pause not working**
- Make sure you're clicking quickly (< 600ms)
- If you hold too long, it switches moods instead
- Try quick, light taps

**Timer not ending**
- Sessions can be 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes
- Visual changes are subtle - look for decreased saturation
- Let it fully fade to white for completion
